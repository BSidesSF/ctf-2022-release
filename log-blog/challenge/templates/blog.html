Recommendations

It's important to choose the right firewood for your needs.

We recommend,

  • Ash
  • Oak
  • Maple

We don't recommend,

  • Pine
  • Eucalyptus
  • Cedar

Signs that your firewood has gone bad:

  • Splitting ends on the logs
  • Log sounds hollow
  • Bark is peeling off
  • Has a wet smell
